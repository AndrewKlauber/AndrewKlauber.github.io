Ayrton Senna

Superhero is a word almost synonymous with the name Ayrton Senna. Ayrton inspired people from all walks of life, backgrounds, and cultures. Ayrton was a hero of Brazil and a hero of racing. Over the course of his racing career, Ayrton donated more than $400 million dollars to charities and impoverished people in Brazil and around the world, but Ayrton never mentioned this publicly.

Ayrton was a brutal and fierce competitor on the racetrack, but in his heart he was a gentle and kind man. He never quit.
